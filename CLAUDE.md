# calendaralarm

An app to make calendar events trigger an alarm that requires effort to silence — to combat the problem of missing appointments despite setting reminders.

## The Problem

Standard clock/alarm apps aren't bad, but calendar reminders are too easy to dismiss. The goal is an alarm that can't be ignored — requiring active effort to cancel, making it much harder to miss important events.
